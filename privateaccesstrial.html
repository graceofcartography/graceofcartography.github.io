Welcome to Mapping Joyscapes

This is a collaborative space for women to digitally archive and share places where they have experienced moments of safety, happiness, and joy.

Click on the map to add your own story. Your contribution helps build a geography of joy.

About Joyscapes

Mapping Joyscapes is a community-generated counter-mapping platform. The platform provides an interface to collaboratively record the cartography of joy and safety—from park benches to the middle of the ocean—in order to preserve our histories and unfolding realities.

Moderation Policy

To ensure this remains a safe and positive space, all submissions are reviewed by a moderator before being published on the map. Submissions that contain hate speech, personally identifiable information, or are otherwise deemed unsafe will be rejected. This process can take up to 48 hours.

How to Contribute

The simplest way to contribute is to add your own story to the map. Simply click anywhere on the map to begin. If you are a developer or designer interested in helping with the project, please reach out via our contact page.
